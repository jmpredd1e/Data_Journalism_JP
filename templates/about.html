About My Project - Jaiden Preddie


One thing that has plauged America since the beginning of its founding is the underlying racial tension.
In recent times these tensions have been most clearly seen in policy brutatility.
So for my data anaylsis I took a look at the races of people who were arrested for RESISTING ARREST.


My first predicament was trying to configure a way for the entire information
document to be spread across a data set. So, since I was talking about New
York and it was winter I used an svg of the different boroughs and used
different brightnesses of blue as an indicator. The different brightnesses of
blue were representative of the black vs non black ratio in each of the
boroughs. In this prototype I had to think of ways to increase user activity
so I added a couple of buttons and a way to go into the more separated drop
down menu similar to how we did in the life expectancy lab.

I figured out how I was going to get to the micro page and I knew how I
formated my reformat data file and using that I thought about using a bar
graph and although the amount of data is not as much as I was hoping it still
had a good amount of data that I could use to draw a conclusion.
